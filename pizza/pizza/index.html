La Gourmet Pizza has proudly been serving Dallas since 1999. "Gourmet" means higher quality or unique, our food lives up to that name. From sandwiches and salads to pizza and pasta, we at LA Gourmet Pizza have it all.  Each pizza begins with our fresh dough that is made daily, we then add only the highest quality ingredients: including our homemade pizza sauce, quality cheeses, lean meats and fresh vegetables.
 
 
Today LA Gourmet Pizza has five locations throughout Kentucky and Texas and we are still growing.  We are grateful to our loyal customers, our employees, and the investors that made this happen.  To all of whom we pledge to remain innovative and unique, and to always custom bake your pizza just the way you like it.
